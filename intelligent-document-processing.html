Frequently Asked Questions

What is Intelligent Document Processing (IDP)?

Intelligent Document Processing uses AI and machine learning to automatically extract, classify, and process data from documents. Unlike traditional OCR, IDP understands document context and can handle unstructured data from invoices, contracts, forms, and more.

What accuracy can IDP achieve?

Our IDP solutions typically achieve 99.2% extraction accuracy. With human-in-the-loop validation using Amazon A2I, we can ensure near-perfect accuracy for critical business processes.

What document types can IDP process?

IDP can process virtually any document type including invoices, contracts, medical records, insurance claims, legal documents, tax forms, purchase orders, ID documents, and more. Our AI adapts to your specific document formats.

How much can IDP reduce document processing costs?

Our clients typically see 60-75% cost savings compared to manual document processing, along with 70% reduction in processing time. ROI is usually achieved within 6-12 months.
