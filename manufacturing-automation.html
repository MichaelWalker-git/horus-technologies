Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI-powered vehicle tracking work?

Our solution uses AWS Textract to extract shipping manifest data from Excel files, then integrates with third-party logistics APIs to provide real-time tracking across trucks and ships. This eliminates manual data entry and provides customers with accurate delivery estimates.

Can you automate translation of technical documentation?

Yes. We use AWS Textract to ingest PDF documents and Amazon Translate for multi-language conversion. This is ideal for safety manuals, compliance documents, and technical specifications that need to be distributed globally.

How does automated purchase order matching work?

Our system uses Textract to extract data from purchase orders, invoices, and receiving documents, then automatically matches and reconciles them. Human-in-the-loop validation (A2I) handles exceptions, ensuring accuracy while dramatically reducing manual effort.

What industries do you serve?

We work with automotive manufacturers (Porsche, Volkswagen), electronics companies (Samtec), and other manufacturing businesses. Our solutions adapt to various supply chain and documentation needs.
